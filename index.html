About

I’m a self-taught designer with over 4 years of experience, who transitioned from freelancing to joining Tech Studio Consult as an in-house designer. I also volunteer as part of the management team at the TechTribe Foundation, fostering community growth and collaboration.

Over the years, I’ve grown significantly, mentored aspiring designers, and refined my approach to create user-first designs that inspire. I also enjoy coding for fun, crafting clean and functional designs purely with HTML and CSS.
